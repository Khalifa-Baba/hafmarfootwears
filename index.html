About Hafmar

Hafmar Footwears

At Hafmar Footwears, we stride with passion and craftsmanship, embodying the vision of our founder, Khalifa-Baba Umar. Since our inception in 2020, we've been committed to delivering footwear that seamlessly blends fashion and comfort. As a pivotal branch of the Hafmar Company, alongside Hafmar Designs, Hafmar C3, Hafmar Computing, Hafmar Kings Collection, and Hafmar Fragrance, we take pride in contributing to a diverse portfolio of excellence. Located in the vibrant city of Kaduna, specifically in Sabon Gari, our roots are deeply embedded in the heart of Nigeria. Hafmar Footwears is more than just a brand; it's a testament to quality craftsmanship and timeless style.

Our product range reflects the diverse preferences of our valued customers. Whether you're seeking trendy half covers, cozy slippers, sophisticated flat ladies' shoes, sturdy boots, or glamorous high heels, Hafmar Footwears has the perfect pair to complement your style. We believe that every step you take should be a confident stride, and that's why we are dedicated to creating footwear that not only looks good but feels incredible. Join us on a journey where each pair of Hafmar shoes tells a story of fashion, comfort, and unmatched quality.

Thank you for choosing Hafmar Footwears – Where Every Step is a Style Statement!

CEO Hafmar Footweras

Khalifa-Baba Umar

Khalifa-Baba Umar, a cobbler with a journey that commenced in 2019 and also a passionate Software Developer. Proud alumnus of Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto, where he honed his skills in Computer Science. At the youthful age of 22. He specialize in Software development using PHP, Python and React.

He also wear the hat of an entrepreneur, steering the ship of some small but mighty ventures, Hafmar Footwears and Hafmar Computing. He learned how to make shoes with AB the Shoe maker who is currently known as 8Twenty8. He started Hafmar Footwears in the year 2020 when the Universities where on Strike. He finished his primary school at Lapan Nursery and Primary school, Kaduna and his secondary school at Betty Queen Int School Kaduna.
